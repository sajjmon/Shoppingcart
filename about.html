About the company

SB Store was founded in Sweden in 2009 by Simon Bergström with the goal of making quality clothes. SB Store designs and markets clothing, wathes, and accessories for men. The company is a 6-time Gaselle award winner following its triple-digit growth and sustained profitability since the brand officially launched in 2012. SB Store is a multiple ISPO design award winner and Brataas is a recipient of the International Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the year award. SB store is independently owned and operated from its headquarters located in stockholm, Sweden where the company employs 20 people.
